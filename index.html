About

Sweet Little Sugar specializes in custom, one-of-a-kind artisan cookies. They make great gifts for everyone from your kiddo's teacher to your mom. They are also awesome favors for birthdays, baby showers, and weddings.

If you're a business owner that would like to promote your company, or say thank you to your clients, cookies are the way to go.

Pricing

Custom decorated cookies are a time-consuming labor of love, and are priced as such. This is a very different product than you'll find at a chain store; to compare the two would be downright silly. Pricing varies, based on size, complexity, and quantity of cookies ordered. I would be honored and humbled to create cookies for you. Contact me.

Terms and Conditions

Thank you for your interest! Now here are some details:

  • An assortment of custom cookies (four shapes, four colors) start at $36/dozen, or $65/two dozen.
  • Favor cookies start at $4 each.
  • I do not reproduce licensed kids characters.
  • All cookies are individually heat-sealed and come on your choice of platter (perfect for parties), or cute box (great for gifting). To individually bag and tie each cookie with a ribbon is an additional $0.50 per cookie.
  • The flavor of my cookies is Vivi's Very Vanilla Bean (Yum!).
  • I am happy to provide an ingredients statement. I do not use nuts in my cookies, but they are produced in my home kitchen, which does contain nuts.

If you'd like to reserve a spot, please let me know, along with the pickup date. Please also attach a copy of the party invite and party decor, if applicable. I will come up with designs and colors to run by you. Please note that these will be rough concepts that might change some once I start decorating. Please place your order at least two weeks in advance. I will confirm with you about a week in advance; if you need to add cookies or make changes, that's the time to tell me. Changes requested after that, if accommodated, may cost more, as all cookies are made to order. If you decide to cancel your event without giving me at least one week's notice, then you've got yourself some cute, delicious cookies to share with your friends. If you have an image for me to use for inspiration, that's great, but please know that I am not going to reproduce someone else's cookies exactly as they were decorated. I do not require a deposit; you can pay at pickup. I spend many hours in the middle of the night putting lots of love into your cookies. If you place an order and do not pick them up, that will make me really sad, and cost me not only the ingredients, and cost of any cutters purchased, but lots of beauty rest, as well (which I desperately need! Seriously, people, can you say "bags"?). Also, if your cookies are a surprise or gift, please tell me so I don't post them to Instagram too soon!

By placing your order, you are acknowledging, and agreeing to, these terms. Now let's make your cookie dreams come true!
