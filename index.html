About

Sweet Little Sugar specializes in custom, one-of-a-kind artisan cookies. They make great gifts for everyone from your kiddo's teacher to your mom. They are also awesome favors for birthdays, baby showers, and weddings.

If you're a business owner that would like to promote your company, or say thank you to your clients, cookies are the way to go.

Pricing

Custom decorated cookies are a time-consuming labor of love, and are priced as such. This is a very different product than you'll find at a chain store; to compare the two would be downright silly. Pricing varies, based on size, complexity, and quantity of cookies ordered. I would be honored and humbled to create cookies for you. Contact me.
